The Great Gatsby

The 1925 novel The Great Gatsby by F.Scott Fitzgerald is a romance turned tragic. In this novel, the reader is Nick Carraway, a new resident in West Egg during the Jazz age. He finds himself becoming friends with his millionaire neighbor Jay Gatsby who throws luxurious parties in hopes his long lost love Daisy Buchanan would arrive. Looking through the lens from Nick Carraway, the reader sees how relationship between Daisy and Gatsby unfold.
